OurSERVICES

WEAVING UNITS

At the heart of our factory lies our state-of-the-art weaving department, equipped with advanced looms that enable us to produce high-quality fabrics with precision and efficiency. Our skilled technicians expertly operate these looms to weave a diverse range of fabrics, from simple to complex designs, with utmost attention to detail and quality. With our weaving looms, we have the flexibility to produce small batches or large volumes, making us a versatile and reliable partner for fabric supply.

WARPING(SECTIONAL)

We use sectional warping to produce high-quality fabrics with precision and efficiency. This technique involves dividing warp yarns into sections, allowing for control over yarn tension, density, and color placement. It enables us to create complex fabrics with exceptional texture, consistency, and pattern accuracy.

PROSESSING (outsource)

While we specialize in weaving and manufacturing high-quality fabrics, we currently outsource our fabric processing to trusted partners. This strategic decision allows us to focus on our core competencies while ensuring that our fabrics receive the best possible finishing treatments. By leveraging the expertise of specialized processing facilities, we guarantee that our fabrics meet the highest standards of quality, texture, and performance.
